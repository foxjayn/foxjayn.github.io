About Me
Product Manager. UX/UI Engineer. Designer.


I am empowered by empowering others. My determination, creativity, and insatiable hunger to learn have guided me to excel despite several unique obstacles, developing the ever-improving leader I am today. I cultivate and harvest the truest happiness from the fulfillment that comes from enlightening others and using deep empathy to improve their quality of life.

I achieved a dual engineering degree at UC Berkeley’s College of Engineering, led a super vehicle engineering and design team, and hosted a hackathon which introduced me to tech. I applied my noteworthy balance of logic and curiosity to succeed as a Product Manager and self-taught Software Engineer at distinguished tech companies. Through this journey, I found passion in empathizing with others, identifying their needs, and creating solutions.

Today’s tech industry has allowed me to reach further than ever before. As a female athlete, engineer, artist, and person of color, I will unveil new methods of self-disruption in the tech industry and emerge superior solutions to the world. My time will be worth that of many.
